Breakthrough and Rise to Fame (2018-Present)


The year 2018 proved to be a turning point. Alec released his mixtape "Narrated for You," featuring songs like "If We Have Each Other," "Water Fountain," "Annabelle's Homework," and the soon-to-be megahit, "Let Me Down Slowly." "Let Me Down Slowly" gained further traction with a re-release featuring singer Alessia Cara. The song became a global success, reaching the top 40 charts in over 25 countries and amassing over 1.7 billion streams on Spotify as of December 2023. To capitalize on the growing popularity, Alec launched the "Can I Sing for You?" video series on YouTube, further connecting with his audience through his engaging personality. Since then, Alec Benjamin has continued to release music, signing with Atlantic Records (2018-2021) and currently with Elektra Records.
